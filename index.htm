  TinyCloud: Cloudlet design on ARM platform
Eugene Bagdasaryan
 

Recent progress on mobile ARM platforms and software virtualization opens new opportunities to utilize this cheap and energy efficient processor as a new platform for virtual clouds. Additionally, cloudlets, servers that are located nearby devices to process information without sending to the cloud, became very popular with the growth of data sent in the systems that implement Internet of Things concept. In this paper we design first cloudlet that runs on ARM platform, uses Xen and Docker containers for isolation. We review design challenges and solutions that were encountered during design and first benchmarking results.
